About the company</strong></p>
Being a member of Holcim Group and one of the largest multinational cement producers inVietnam, we are a dynamic organization and looking for proactive, energetic and results driven individuals to join the Holcim team.

Established in 1994, HolcimVietnamcurrently employs around 1,500 people at its 4 technologically advanced cement production sites and modern ready-mix concrete batching plants.  Our core businesses can be categorized into three groups:

1. Cement: </strong>In response to the increasing demands of construction industry, HolcimVietnam has developed a wide cement portfolio to better meet the requirements specification applications. The network of 4 cement plants in strategic locations in the south ensures the best logistic service and uninterrupted supply.

2. Ready-mix concrete: </strong>Holcim has always strived to further meet the different requirements of customers. With innovative solutions and services, Holcim has been trusted by many national and international leading contractors and investors; and became one of the leading ready-mix supplier in the southernVietnam.

3. Geocycle: </strong>Holcim provides safe and sustainable waste management solutions for many industrial waste types through co-processing in cement kilns. Holcim has 20 years of professional and experience expertise in Alternative Fuel Resourcing in over 40 countries.

Why join Holcim Vietnam?</strong>

A people development culture:</strong> with processes to attract and retain the right talent; build leadership capabilities that can lead in change and the culture we want; and motivate and engage employees to give off their best.

Staff with the right attitude for high performance</strong>: those who do not wait to be told what to do; willing to operate out of comfort zone, high capacity to learn and drive results forward without stopping at the first obstacle.

International standards of operations:</strong> with modern equipment and systems; strong processes and controls; world-class technical expertise; good Corporate Social Responsibility; strict environmental compliance and high safety standards.

An open and warm working environment:</strong> through coaching; New Ideas program (Y Kien Moi), employee engagement surveys; observing and learning from each other’s’ job (Day in Life Of); and tools to equip teams with a healthy team dynamics and to improve performance.

Opportunities for learning and development:</strong> with structured On the Job training, coaching and mentoring, technical training, soft skills training, leadership and supervisory development programs, regional and global programs.

Competitive benefits:</strong> such as modern office in Ho Chi Minh City and high-tech plant facilities; year end and team celebrations; corporate social responsible activities; housing and location allowances (where applicable); and attractive package.
