Job description:</strong>

Our company is seeking Game Developers to join our Game Studio. The Tamtay Game studio is one of the leading studios in Vietnam. You can join in a very modern game developing process and help us build many challenging and interesting games.

Job responsibilities:</strong>

• Develop new games – client side or server side.

Job requirements:</strong>

• Degree in Information Technology / Computer Science or related fields.

• Creative thinking.
• Knowledge of game servers: SmartFox, Electro, FlashMedia Server… is a big advantage.
• Good teamwork skills.
• Can work under high pressure.

Benefits:</strong>

- Competitive salary.
- Free lunch.
- Health benefits.
- Regular team building activities.
- Young, friendly and dynamic international working environment.

Location:</strong> Hanoi, Vietnam
