Job descriptions (or duties)</strong>:         </strong>

1/ Accounting management:
- Prepare payment and collection report by weekly and monthly.
- Prepare monthly and yearly financial statements: balance sheet, income statement, cash flow.
- Responsible for preparation of all taxes (VAT, CIT, PIT,…) and other reports required by governmental.
- Follow up the payment of transfer by bank and cash.
- Prepare monthly Petty Cash Reconciliation, Bank Account reconciliation.
- Analyze cost components and variances comparing actual cost vs estimate cost.
- Handling the budgeting, cash flow management and KPI on monthly, quarterly and yearly, budget versus actual analysis of each project/ campaign.
- Participate in doing Plan/budget as assignment.
2/ HR management: payroll, labor contracts, employee data, register insurance to associates
