Job description:</strong>

We’re looking for Game Designers to join us. You will work collaboratively in small teams that are focused on making games that push technological and creative boundaries. - Trust worthy and reliable
- Patient, perfection-oriented, well-organized with excellent time management
- Good social/interactive skills and a good team player.
