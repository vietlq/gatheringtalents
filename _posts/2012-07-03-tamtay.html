Job description:</strong>

Tamtay Jsc is a leading Internet company in VN. We are seeking for a very Senior Web designer to lead complex and challenging web projects. The Senior Designer is responsible for the creation of all online visual design, including typography, visual concept, logo and icon design for the Internet and interactive platforms.

Job responsibilities:</strong>

- Design original concepts mockup pages graphically to illustrate the nature of the web experience.
- Creating a front-end design that is unique in terms of branding and sophistication yet in synch with the overall design.
- Ensure usability, interface design and interaction design standards are adhered to and applied throughout the website.
- Think strategically about new design paradigms and new features sets.
- Document interface designs for communication to developers.
- Maintain knowledge of current trends, color and technology relevant to web and graphic design.
- Demonstrate knowledge of XHTML, email production, web standards, current web technologies and browser compatibility..
- Understanding of website optimization

Job requirements:</strong>

- Can work under high pressure, keen to learn and be a self motivator
- Trust worthy and reliable
- Patient, perfection-oriented, well-organized with excellent time management
- Good social/interactive skills and a good team player.
- 4+ years of recent web and graphic design experience
- Degree in Web Design and / or related discipline is preferred.

Benefits:</strong>

- Competitive salary
- Free lunch
- Health support
- Regular team building activities
- Young, friendly and dynamic international working environment.

Location:</strong> Hanoi, Vietnam
