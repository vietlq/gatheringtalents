About the company:</strong>

Misfit Wearables was founded by the founders of Agamatrix- the world leading company in blood glucose testing device and Apple&Pepsi Former CEO John Sculley. Misfit is developing highly wearable sensor products and services for wellness and medical applications. The device will be wearable and can measure human vital signs.
Misfit Wearables currently has two offices: Software R&D center in Ho Chi Minh and Hardware center in San Francisco. In April 2012, Misfit Wearables received its 1st funding from Founders Fund and Khosla Ventures: http://techcrunch.com/2012/04/27/the-wearable-computing-startup-from-agamatrixs-founders-former-apple-ceo-john-sculley-just-raised-7-6m/</a>
