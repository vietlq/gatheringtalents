The candidate will be responsible to deliver design and development of solutions and product configuration in the Integrated order management system of CRM domain. He or she will be responsible for the timeliness, accuracy and quality of the solutions and configuration delivered following a full SDLC process where time-to-market is critical.

  • Design, develop and provide technical leadership in the delivery and deployment of solutions and configuration, ensuring timeliness and assured quality.</li>
  • Collaborate with Solution Architect, Business Analysts and other IS teams, and vendor in arriving at recommendations for technical decisions in the delivery of detailed designs and resolution of design issues at application or database level.</li>
  • Assist in project planning, technical feasibility study, project scoping, solutioning and effort estimation.</li>
  • Develop, review and execute test plans, test scenarios and test cases for functional testing.</li>
  • Perform impact analysis on reported incidents, deliver fixes (if any) within agreed SLA</li>

  • Perform training & maintain the solution documentation</li>
  • Lead process improvement initiatives and make appropriate recommendations to enable productivity gains</li>
  • Provide progress updates and escalate issues on a timely basis</li>
