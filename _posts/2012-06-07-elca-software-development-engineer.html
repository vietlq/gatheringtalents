Responsibilities:</strong>

As a Software Development Engineer you will be in a team with other highly motivated engineers.
You will actively participate in the development of software applications for our customers.
Supported by state-of-the-art methods and technologies, you will have opportunities to be involved in the technical aspects of the complete project life-cycle, from requirements clarification and technical design to coding, testing, integration and deployment.

We offer:</strong>

- Professional working environment
- Projects using state-of-the-art technologies
- Extensive coaching and training
- Attractive remuneration packages
- Comfortable workplaces
- Travel opportunities

Requirements:</strong>

You hold a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information and Communications Technology of Software Engineering, and maybe already have a first work experience in software development for the Java or .NET platform, for which you have a very strong interest in software design, programming and testing.
Being technically confident, flexibility, a strong team spirit, as well as good reading skills in English are essential for the job.

Company information:</strong>

ELCA is an independent Swiss IT Services Company and a leader in software development, system integration and business consulting. ELCA has helped its clients to leverage information technology innovations successfully for over 40 years. The company has 500 employees, more than 400 of whom are highly skilled engineers. With its headquarters in Lausanne, ELCA has subsidiaries in Zurich, Geneva, Berne, London, Paris, Madrid and Ho-Chi-Minh City (offshore development).
ELCA Vietnam plays a strategic role to maintain the group’s competiveness. Our continuous success is based on a strong commitment to quality and project management in combination with technological leadership and thorough business understanding. With our Quality System appraised at CMMI-ML3, we are able to deliver and maintain customized software for a large number of customers.
