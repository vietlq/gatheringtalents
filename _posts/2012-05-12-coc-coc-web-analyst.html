Job Description:</strong>

Offering valuable content to user is the critical mission of a search engine. However websites often include duplicated content or meaningless information (trash pages), which affect performance of the system.

A web analyst is responsible for investigation of uninformative pages and distinguishing them from valuable contents. He/she leads filtering of trash pages and works with freelancers and developers.

Responsibilities:</strong>

  • Propose strategy to filter meaningless pages efficiently;</li>
  • Manage small group of freelancers (3-5 people) to provide raw data;</li>
  • Make decisions based on freelancers’ reports and his/her own statistics, provide them to developers;</li>
  • Interact with developers for data update, automation or other related technical issues.</li>
    </ul>
    Requirements:</strong>

    • Analytical skill;</li>
    • Knowledge in core web technologies, such as Http, Html;</li>
    • Fluent English;</li>
    • Explanation and management skill;</li>
    • Experience in regular expressions;</li>
    • Basic experience in Unix shell, including tools: Grep, Awk, Cut, Sort.</li>
      </ul>
      Advantages:</strong>

      • Knowledge of common algorithms, data structures and complexity of algorithms;</li>
      • Experience in Java SE;</li>
      • Knowledge of scripting languages on basic level: PERL, PHP and Python.</li>
        </ul>

        • Competitive salary;</li>

          </ul>
