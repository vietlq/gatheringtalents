*About the team:</strong>

External Relation is the frontline team of Enterprise Family, a group of young and passionate people who are well bonded together by the mission of creating an entrepreneurship platform to connect Vietnamese entrepreneurs around the world and strengthening the bilateral commercial relationship between Singapore and Vietnam.

In External Relation team, we initiate and develop strategic plans to connect Enterprise Family with external parties including investors, businesses, entrepreneurs and high profile professionals. We establish strategic relationships with existing and prospective stakeholders and continuously broaden our network base. We convey the mission of Enterprise Family to the business community and generate new opportunities for all by connecting and synergising resources and expertises.

*Scope of work</strong>

As part of our team, you will have the chance to interact with companies, organizations and high profile businessmen. You will need to identify potential members/ partners and sponsors for Enterprise Family in Vietnam as well as Singapore and initiate effective plans to establish strategic relationships with these companies and organizations.
You have to be proactive in generating new contacts and setting up meetings with new prospects.

You will need to work closely with the team to understand thoroughly the value that Enterprise Family is aspired to generate for our prospective stakeholders, initiate new approaches and suggest improvements to our existing practices.
