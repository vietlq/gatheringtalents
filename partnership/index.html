Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry (VCCI)</strong>

VCCI is an independent, non-governmental, non-profit organization representing the business community in Vietnam. The objectives of VCCI are to assist the development of the Vietnamese enterprises as well as to promote the commercial cooperation between Vietnam and the rest of the world on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, contributing to the sustainable socio-economic growth in Vietnam.

The Singapore Business Group (SBG)</strong>

SGB is the leading group in promoting the mutual business development between Singapore and Vietnam. Starting from a small group of 30 Singaporeans doing business in Vietnam, SBG has become an established non-profit organization with more than 300 members. The organization has actively involved in connecting businesses from both Vietnam and Singapore through various networking sessions and seminars.

Vietnamese Community in the National University of Singapore (VNCNUS)</strong>

VNCNUS is a vibrant student community comprising of more than 1,000 Vietnamese undergraduates and alumni in the National University of Singapore. The community organises annual cultural event, music event, volunteer project and career fair, enriching members’ personal development as well as career opportunities.
