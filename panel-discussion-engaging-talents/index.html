The outlook for the predicted growth in Vietnam comes with the challenge of productivity to sustain it. Thus, the demand for more international experienced professionals in the workforce is increasing. However, how to engage overseas Vietnamese talents in thriving Vietnam economy remains a difficult question for most corporations.

This discussion panel provides a platform for companies to understand candidates’ expectations and at the same time, share their concerns when recruiting overseas professionals.
